<h2 class="about-greeting">Hi, I am Yongjeong Oh</h2>

I am a Research Fellow at the **[Singapore University of Technology and Design (SUTD)](https://www.sutd.edu.sg/)**, working with **[Prof. Jihong Park](https://sites.google.com/view/jihong-park)**.

I received my Ph.D. and M.S. in Electrical Engineering from
**[Pohang University of Science and Technology (POSTECH)](https://postech.ac.kr/eng/)**, under the supervision of **[Prof. Yo-Seb Jeon](https://wcml.postech.ac.kr/)**.
Before that, I received my B.S. in Electronics Engineering from **[Pusan National University (PNU)](https://www.pusan.ac.kr/eng/Main.do)**.

My research interests lie at the intersection of wireless communications and deep learning.
In particular, I am interested in the following topics:

<h3 class="about-research-heading"><i class="fa-solid fa-book-open"></i>Research Interests</h3>

<div class="research-pills">
  <span class="research-pill">Semantic Communications</span>
  <span class="research-pill">AI-Native Air Interface</span>
  <span class="research-pill">Distributed Learning</span>
</div>
